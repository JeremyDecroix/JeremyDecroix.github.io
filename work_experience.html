Work Experience

With a diverse background spanning aerospace research, startup mentorship, and advanced manufacturing, I've had the privilege of collaborating with esteemed institutions and spearheading transformative projects. Here are some highlights from my professional journey:

Aerospace Systems Design Laboratory (ASDL)

As a Graduate Researcher at the Aerospace Systems Design Laboratory (ASDL), I have the privilege of working under the expert guidance of Prof. Dimitri Mavris. My experience at ASDL provided me with the opportunity to delve deep into cutting-edge research and innovation. I had the honor of consulting and generating state-of-the-art research that significantly contributed to projects commissioned by prestigious entities such as NASA, the FAA, and the Atlanta International Airport. This invaluable exposure not only honed my technical acumen but also enabled me to understand the intricacies of aerospace design and its real-world applications.

Starburst Aerospace Accelerator & UCLA

During my internship, I had the incredible opportunity to pioneer a 13-week early-stage startup program called SCALE and designed to empower 16 budding entrepreneurs. This ambitious initiative paved the way for them to launch and scale their ventures, with a collective achievement of securing over $3M in funding.

One of the most significant events I organized was a Demo Day at UCLA, which drew the attendance of over 60 investors, underlining the program's wide-reaching impact and potential. In addition to event management, I played an instrumental role in ensuring the entrepreneurs had the best guidance by managing mentor sessions and assisting with more than 100 key introductions. This experience not only honed my organizational skills but also reinforced my passion for fostering entrepreneurial spirit and innovation.

Safran Electronics & Defense

During my internship as an Aeronautical Production Technical Assistant at Safran Electronics and Defense, I undertook significant responsibilities that yielded profound impacts on operational efficiencies. One of my proudest accomplishments was orchestrating the rollout of the 5S management methods. This strategic implementation transformed our assembly line operations, resulting in an impressive 20% increase in efficiency for over 200 technicians.

Additionally, I championed a comprehensive redesign of our workstation management, ensuring a more streamlined and productive workspace. Complementing this, I also spearheaded an extensive industrial tooling indexing campaign that meticulously cataloged and organized over 700 tools, paving the way for better tool management and reduced downtime. This experience has equipped me with a profound understanding of aeronautical production processes and the importance of continuous improvement in a high-tech, fast-paced environment.
