Education

Traversing the world of engineering from the heart of France to the cutting-edge labs at Georgia Tech, my academic journey has been fueled by a passion for sustainable innovation and a dedication to redefining the boundaries of aerospace engineering.

Georgia Tech | Ph.D. Aerospace Engineering

As an aerospace engineering PhD student at Georgia Tech, I am proud to be a part of the Aerospace Systems Design Laboratory (ASDL) where I am conducting cutting-edge research towards a zero emission aviation by 2050. My focus is on the design of a multi fuel hybrid engine, which is a crucial step in the direction of sustainable aviation.

My research aims to find a balance between the performance and environmental impact of aircraft by exploring the use of alternative fuels in combination with conventional ones. My goal is to bring us one step closer to realizing a future of sustainable air travel. I am dedicated to finding innovative solutions that will benefit the environment and the industry and push the boundaries of what is possible in the field of aerospace engineering.

Georgia Tech | M.Sc. Aerospace Engineering

During my tenure at Georgia Tech, where I pursued a Master of Science in Aerospace Engineering, I undertook intensive coursework in Optimization, Advanced Design Methods, Aircraft Design, Aerospace Systems Engineering, and Optimal Guidance & Control. This academic rigor was complemented by a Full-Tuition Research Assistantship at the Aerospace Systems Design Laboratory (ASDL) sponsored by esteemed organizations such as NASA and the FAA. Furthermore, I earned a Certificate in International Security & Aerospace Systems from the Sam Nunn School of International Affairs, signifying my well-rounded expertise in the domain.

A significant portion of my graduate journey was dedicated to ASDL, where I contributed to landmark research projects with profound impacts on the aerospace sector. Notably, I was part of the Electric Propulsion Flight Demonstrator project applying hybrid electric propulsion modeling; the Thin-Haul RAM Operation Optimization focusing on fleet design; the Atlanta Aerotropolis City Concept implementing Agent-Based Simulation modeling and crafting a Digital Twin for airport traffic; and the Supersonic Inlets Aero-Mechanical Design where I devised algorithms for supersonic inlet designs. These experiences have greatly shaped my perspective, honed my skills, and deepened my passion for aerospace engineering.

Arts & Metiers | M.Sc. Industrial Engineering

From 2018 to 2020, I undertook an intensive and comprehensive education at the esteemed Arts et Métiers ParisTech in Paris, France, where I successfully completed my Master and Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering. My academic journey was enriched by a diverse curriculum that included but was not limited to Electronics & Controls, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Solid Mechanics, Structures, Materials Science, and Fluid Dynamics. I was also honored to be the recipient of the Louis Magne Academic Excellence Scholarship, a testament to my dedication in my academic pursuits. Moreover, I actively participated in student representation, serving as a voice and advocate for over 1,200 fellow students at the institution.

Ginette | B.Sc. Mechanical Engineering

During my time at the prestigious Lycée Sainte Geneviève, I undertook a rigorous two-year multidisciplinary engineering program. This intensive learning experience was specifically tailored to prepare students like myself for the entrance exams to France's top-ranking Graduate Engineering Schools. My curriculum was comprehensive, delving deep into core engineering concepts. Among the subjects I mastered were Thermodynamics, Electromagnetism, Linear Algebra, Fluid Mechanics, and Mechanical Engineering. Beyond my academic endeavors, I held a key leadership role as the Treasurer of the Student Association, where I was responsible for managing and allocating funds, enhancing my skill set in both teamwork and organizational management. This holistic educational experience has equipped me with a solid foundation in both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, ready to be applied in my next academic and professional pursuits.
